## How to succeed in this course (short version)

1. **Follow the order.** Use the [Course outline](course-outline/) and [Learning path](learning-path/). Start with prerequisites and math if you need them; then Volume 1 (foundations, bandits, MDPs, DP), then Volume 2 (MC, TD, SARSA, Q-learning), then Volume 3 and beyond. Do not skip the foundations.

2. **Do the exercises.** Each chapter has one exercise and a worked solution (collapsible). Try the exercise first; then compare with the solution. Re-implement later without looking to solidify the material.

3. **Use the assessments.** Take the [Preliminary assessment](preliminary/) to see if you are ready for the curriculum. Use the phase quizzes ([Phase 1 math](assessment/phase-1-math/), [Phase 2 readiness](assessment/phase-2-readiness/), [Phase 3 foundations](assessment/phase-3-foundations/), [Phase 4 Deep RL](assessment/phase-4-deep-rl/)) to check progress.

4. **Code regularly.** Even 20–30 minutes per day helps. Consistency beats cramming.

5. **When stuck:** Re-read the chapter, check the [Course outline](course-outline/) for related topics, try the solution for that part only, or see the Appendix for [How to Code by Yourself](appendix/how-to-code-by-yourself-1/) and [How to Succeed (Long Version)](appendix/how-to-succeed-long/).

For more detail, see the [Appendix: How to Succeed in this Course (Long Version)](appendix/how-to-succeed-long/) and the [Learning path](learning-path/).
